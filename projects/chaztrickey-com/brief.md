### 9. Visual Direction

Color palette:
  Patriotic but refined — not kitsch. Deep navy, crimson red,
  clean white, warm amber/gold (referencing the flame in the
  tattoo). Hints of firework energy in subtle animations or
  background details. Should feel premium, not costume-shop.

Aesthetic feel:
  Dark background editorial — like a serious publication that
  also knows how to look alive. Think: the credibility of ink
  on paper meets the energy of a live broadcast.

Animation style:
  Scroll-driven storytelling (jeton.com reference). Sections
  that animate into view, content that reveals as you scroll.
  Lively without being distracting. Motion serves the content,
  not the other way around.

Inspiration sites:
  1. https://www.jeton.com — scroll interactions, dark bg,
     premium feel, sections that come alive as you move down
