Who is Chaz Trickey?

A senior IT architect, a faith-led family man, and a teacher at heart trying to make complicated things a little more understandable.

I have spent more than twenty years working with technology: computers, networks, software, data, security, and the kind of systems people depend on even when they do not think about them.

That background matters, but it is not the whole point. The point is teaching. I want regular people to understand the tools shaping their lives without feeling talked down to or left behind.

I also care deeply about America, faith, family, and the way people live when the cameras are off. The civic and values content on this site will be honest and direct, but it will not be built on hate or performance.

This site is the official home for that work: technology made simple, grounded commentary, and the values that hold the rest together.
