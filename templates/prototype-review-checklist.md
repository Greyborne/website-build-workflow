## Client Review Questions

- Does the first impression feel right for your business or brand?
- Does the homepage make it clear who you help and what visitors should do next?
- Does the writing sound like you?
- Are the most important sections in the right order?
- Is anything missing that a visitor must know before contacting, following, or buying?
- Is anything unnecessary, distracting, or out of scope?
- Do the colors, type, imagery, and spacing feel appropriate?
- Are placeholder photos, logos, or content acceptable for now?
- Which page or section needs the most revision?
- What must be changed before production build starts?

## Internal Review

- [ ] Prototype matches the approved brief.
- [ ] Prototype uses the approved design system.
- [ ] Prototype follows the page blueprints or documents intentional deviations.
- [ ] Prototype avoids generic layout/copy anti-patterns.
- [ ] Prototype works at mobile, tablet, and desktop widths.
- [ ] Prototype has no obvious accessibility blockers.
- [ ] Prototype uses honest placeholder and coming-soon states.
- [ ] Prototype does not include CMS/backend/database work.

## Verdict

- [ ] Approved for production build
- [ ] Approved with minor revisions
- [ ] Needs design/content revision before production build
